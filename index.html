July 4th , 2019 will be the start of our paddle to Lummi, Washington and we will leave Port Alberni and paddle to Tseshaht territory in the Broken group islands where we will welcome other Nuu-Chah-Nulth canoes to our traditional lands.

We are fairly new to tribal journeys, this is Tseshaht' 3rd year in journeys. Our first year we paddled to Campbell River and Last year We paddled to Puyalup Washington. We welcome anyone who wants to be a part of our family. We do lots of fundraisers through out the year, we practice paddling and attend special events locally.

We will need

  • Money
  • Paddlers
  • Land crew
  • Drivers
  • Support boat driver
